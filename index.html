Collaborative Telepresence


“Imagine a group of people in different parts of the world smoothly interacting as if they were physically together, down to being able to feel one another’s touch. The components that will enable such “collaborative telepresence” could transform how we work and play together, rendering physical location irrelevant." (Corinna E. Lathan, Andrew Maynard, 2019)

What is Collaborative Telepresence?

This is a number of technologies that allow its user to feel like they are present and give the manifestation of one being present.
according to eZTalks.

Collaborative Telepresence is one of the most advanced form where persons can communicate through visual and audio experiences and helps reduce the cost of travel expenses and the time lost through commuting.
